Quick Help: Progress Bar

  

 

Mouse down on progress bar

Left Mouse DownGo to mouse position
Right Mouse DownStart/Pause playing
Shift+Mouse DownGo to beginning

 

Keys for audio/video preview

Space barStart/Pause playing
LeftFrame step backward (Video only)
Shift+Left1 second backward
RightFrame step forward (Video only)
Shift+Right1 second forward
Shift+Space barGo to beginning
