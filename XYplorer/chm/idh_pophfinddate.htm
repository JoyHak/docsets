Quick Help: Find Files by Date

  

 

"between"-field

Absolute date, e.g. 17.02.2005 20:26:24 or 17.02.2005

or

[Empty] equivalent to the date/time now.

 

"and/add"-field

Absolute date, e.g. 17.02.2005 20:26:24 or 17.02.2005

or

[Empty] equivalent to the date/time now.

or

Relative date, e.g.

5 s = "between"-date plus 5 seconds
21 n = "between"-date plus 21 minutes (yes, "n")
4 h = "between"-date plus 4 hours
1 d = "between"-date plus 1 day ("d" is optional)
-15 D = "between"-date minus 15 workdays (big "D")
-9 w = "between"-date minus 9 weeks
-3 m = "between"-date minus 3 months
-10 y = "between"-date minus 10 years
